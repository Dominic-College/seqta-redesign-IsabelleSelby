Enviroment Club

Helping the school reduce its carbon footprint.

New members welcome!

Meetings are every Friday in STEAM room. In meetings we discuss the plans for the future of the school and how we can improve the school now. We chat, discuss and share ideas and talk about how your ideas can come to life. You are welcome to come and find out what it's all about.
If you need more information talk to one of the current members or talk to Mr O'Leary via Mr O'Leary's Email.

Why and how we are helping the school reduce its carbon footprint.

Why?

  • We aim to help the school lessen our impact on the environment
  • Help to lessen the impact on the environment globally
  • To be environmental leaders in the local community
  • We need to start thinking now to have an effect in the future

How?

  • Taking a scientific approach
  • Narrowing ideas
  • Focusing on litter around school
  • Undertaking surveys
  • Introducing morning clean ups
  • Advertising
  • Recycling bins will be introduced
